How to play
  1. Click a piece in the tray to select it.
  2. Hover the board to preview — green = valid, red = invalid.
  3. Click to place. 6+ same-colored blocks touching clear together for points.
  4. Gravity shifts to a random direction after every move (↓ ← ↑ →).
  5. All loose blocks slide fully in the new gravity direction.
  6. Game ends when no piece fits anywhere. Survive!
